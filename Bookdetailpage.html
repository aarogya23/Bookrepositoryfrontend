Summary

Origin is a 2017 mystery thriller novel by American author Dan Brown. It is the fifth installment in the Robert Langdon series, following previous books about the Harvard symbologist. The novel is set in Spain and follows Langdon as he investigates a discovery that could change the future of science and religion.

People in Glass Houses by Jayne Castle is a futuristic tale for Jayne Ann Krentz fans, a sequel to After Dark. Elinor Merritt is reunited with psychic abilities but in harmony with advanced technology. The story follows the main characters, Emmett and Elinor as they drawn together under unusual circumstances.

Emmett, a disgraced psychic, works for a corporation that offers psychic services to a futuristic society. When she finds herself trapped in a dangerous situation involving a mysterious conspiracy, she seeks the help of Sam, a former investigator with a dark past. As they uncover the secrets surrounding a glass house, a mysterious structure that conceals both past and present dangers, Emmett must navigate the growing attraction while hiding hidden dangers.

The novel combines elements of mystery, suspense, and romance, with a focus on psychic abilities, futuristic technology, and the connections that bind people together. Glass Houses explores themes of trust, betrayal, and love with the futuristic setting providing a unique backdrop for the story.
